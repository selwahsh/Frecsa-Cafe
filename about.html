Our Story

Me & my husband, have been living in Newbury for 7 years, and we love food and coffee and always felt that Newbury was missing the Mediterranean feel, so we thought to bring it to the table and open up a Mediterranean style CoffeeShop, where we can offer exciting and freshly prepared food, come visit us and try our amazing choices of fresh salads, or enjoy a nice selection of sweet Mediterranean baklava with our freshly ground locally roasted coffee
